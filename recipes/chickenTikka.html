chickenTikka

The image shows a delicious chicken tikka dish, known for its vibrant spices and rich flavors. It's a popular Indian appetizer that's marinated in a blend of spices and yogurt, then grilled to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thighs
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Turmeric powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Oil or melted butter

Headings

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the yogurt, lemon juice, ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, turmeric powder, chili powder, garlic, ginger, and salt to create the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken chunks to the marinade and coat them evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight for best results.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers.
  4. Grill the chicken for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  5. Remove from the grill and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve hot with your favorite side dishes.
