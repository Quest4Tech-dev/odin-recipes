butterChicken

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 can (400 grams) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoons ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt and lemon juice. Add the chicken pieces and coat them evenly with the mixture. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight for best results.
  2. In a large skillet or pan, heat the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until it turns golden brown.
  3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes to the pan and stir well. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the tomato mixture thickens slightly.
  5. Stir in the ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt. Cook for another 2 minutes to blend the spices.
  6. Add the marinated chicken pieces to the pan. Cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and fully tender.
  7. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the heavy cream. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the sauce to simmer and thicken.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala over the dish and mix well.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
  10. Serve hot with naan or rice.
